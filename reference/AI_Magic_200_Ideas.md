# AI Magic for Moms: 200 Things You Can Actually DO with AI
## Tutorial & Content Ideas — Concept and Principle Based

**Purpose:** Master idea list for AI Vault content. These are organized by category and designed to persist across platforms — teaching concepts, principles, and transferable skills rather than "click here on this website." When a tutorial references a specific tool, the underlying skill should work on any equivalent tool.

**Principle:** Every item answers the question "What can I MAKE or DO?" not "What should I KNOW?"

## PRINCIPLES THAT THREAD THROUGH EVERYTHING

These are the transferable skills that persist regardless of which AI platform is trending this month:

- **Prompt engineering as a communication skill** — learning to be clear, specific, and context-rich in how you ask for things (useful with AI AND humans)
- **Consistent character creation** — the technique of maintaining visual consistency across multiple AI generations
- **Iterative refinement** — the principle of starting rough and improving, not waiting for perfection
- **Context loading** — the skill of giving AI enough background about YOUR specific situation to get personalized results
- **Output evaluation** — developing the eye to know when AI output is good, when it's close, and when it's garbage
- **Ethical awareness** — understanding what's OK to create, what crosses lines, and how to think about AI-generated content responsibly
- **Multi-tool thinking** — knowing that different AI tools have different strengths and choosing the right tool for the job
- **Building on AI output** — using AI as a starting point, not an ending point, and adding your own creativity and judgment
- **Teaching others** — the skill of taking something you figured out and making it learnable for someone else
- **Seeing possibilities** — training yourself to look at any problem or desire and ask "could AI help with this?"
